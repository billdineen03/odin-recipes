Quick "Cold-Brew" Iced Coffee

Description

Want cold-brew iced coffee but don't want to wait overnight? If you have a Chemex style brewer, you're in luck!

Ingredients

  • 40g coffee of your choice, freshly ground to medium fineness
  • 240g ice
  • 422g freshly boiled water

Instructions

  1. Add ice to brewer.
  2. Pre-rinse filter with hot water then place filter in top of brewer.
  3. Add ground coffee to filter, then pour over about 100g of water. Wait 20 seconds.
  4. Continue to add water, about 100g at a time, waiting 10-20 seconds after each addition (you're waiting for the water to pass through the grounds). Add 422g of water total.
  5. Serve immediately in a glass with fresh ice or pour into a storage container and refrigerate until ready to use.
